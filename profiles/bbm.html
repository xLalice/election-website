Bio

Ferdinand "Bongbong" Romualdez Marcos Jr., commonly known as Bongbong Marcos, is a Filipino politician and the son of former Philippine President Ferdinand Marcos and former First Lady Imelda Marcos. He was born on September 13, 1957, in Manila, Philippines.

Marcos started his political career in 1980 when he was elected as the Vice Governor of Ilocos Norte, his family's stronghold. He later served as the Governor of Ilocos Norte from 1983 to 1986. However, his family's rule was cut short when his father's regime was overthrown during the People Power Revolution in 1986.
